Minecraft Zoo Event
ft. Toronto Zoo

April 11 - April 17

Money raised: $840
Registered participants: 120
Workshops: 4

The Minecraft Zoo Event marks the kickoff of PassionFruit Youth. We hosted a variety informative and fun workshops in collaboration with guest speakers from the Toronto Zoo Wildlife Conservancy. This event was centered around a Minecraft build competition where participants put together beautiful Minecraft builds of animals alongside a habitat for the animals.
